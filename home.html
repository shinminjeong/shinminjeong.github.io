Projects

Roslingifier: Semi-Automated Storytelling for Animated Scatterplots
Roslingifier is a semi-automatic storytelling system based on techniques derived from an analysis of data presentation videos. Roslingifier supports quick prototyping of data presentation with auto-detected events and enabled storytelling techniques, such as gesture, visual effects, and animation playback. Our experimental results indicate that Roslingifier supports users in effectively creating data presentations that attract audiences using highlighted events and narratives.
Visualising Influence in Networks of Time Series
AttentionFlow is a system to visualise networks of time series and the dynamic influence they have on one another. Centred around an ego node, our system simultaneously presents the time series on each node. It supports interactions such as overlaying time series of influence, and filtering neighbours by time or flux.
Influence Flowers of Academic Entities
The Influence Flower is a novel data abstraction on an interactive system (InfluenceFlower) which visualises the academic influence through citation network. It breakdowns the influence between individuals, organisations and topics, and provides qualitative comparison over time.
